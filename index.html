How to make an M&M cookie

M&M cookies are a favorite treat for many people. They're a fun twist on the classic chocolate chip cookie, and they're a great use for leftover candy! This recipe can be used to make soft and chewy cookies or cripsy crunchy cookies depending on your preference. Explore this page to see the required ingredients and directions to make this perfect cookie.

1 cup butter

2/3 cup brown sugar

2/3 cup sugar

1 egg

1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

1 1/4 tsp baking soda

1 tsp salt

1 3/4 cups M&Ms

2 cups flour
